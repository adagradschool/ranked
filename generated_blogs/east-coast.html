Pizza Chains in New York and Miami: Discover Lucali

Lucali: A Love for Pizza

Welcome to Lucali, a renowned pizza restaurant celebrated for its commitment to quality and authentic flavors. Founded by Mark Iacono, Lucali began as a labor of love to preserve the local candy store of his Carroll Gardens youth. Today, it proudly serves delicious pizzas in both Brooklyn, New York, and Miami Beach, Florida.

New York Location: A Slice of Brooklyn

Located at 575 Henry Street, Brooklyn, NY, Lucali offers a cozy dining experience with a “first come, first served” policy. Known for its made-to-order pizzas, you can indulge in our large basil pie or calzones, all made with fresh, quality ingredients.

Miami Location: Taste of the Beach

Lucali has brought its acclaimed pizza to Miami Beach, located at 1930 Bay Rd, Miami Beach, FL. With a laid-back atmosphere and a menu that includes signature pies and calzones, it's the perfect spot to enjoy a slice by the ocean. We offer unique toppings and a variety of side dishes to satisfy all cravings.
